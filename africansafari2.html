African Safari

Don't be surprised if you hear tribal drums or even a lion's call.

Intricate stonework is formed around the jacuzzi hot tub, creating the mood of an African jungle. A grass thatched hut overhangs the bed area and extends over the hot tub. Handcrafted statues and artwork from Nairobi and Kenya hang on the wall and are placed on shelves around the room, creating a sense of earth and jungle. Open the door to your large ivory white steam/shower unit with stereo marine speakers inside, billowing hot steam immediately soothing tense muscles. Listen and enjoy your favourite music on our 200 AMP Stereo with CD player or snuggle up to a movie with our 50" Flat Screen TV with DVD player.
