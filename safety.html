Safety Training Programs

Clearview Rentals offers a lift operator training certification for the Skyjack series. Safety is extremely important to us and we would like to ensure that all users feel safe and comfortable on our lifts. We provide training for both electric and rough terrain lifts. Each 3-hour course consists of both lift knowledge and application of the information.

Please contact us to inquire about training dates.

Equipment Maintenance

As soon as we receive a lift at the warehouse, it goes through a complete safety inspection performed by our highly skilled technician. Every part of the machine is thoroughly inspected and tested to ensure that the next lift you rent excels in performance and safety standards. Each inspected lift is marked with a safety tag for your insurance, as your safety is our top priority.

Daily Operator Checklist:

Before each use, we ask that all operators inspect and test all items on this checklist to ensure safety while using our rented equipment:

  • Operating and emergency controls
  • Safety devices and limit switches
  • Personal protective devices
  • Tires and wheels
  • Outriggers (if applicable) and other structures
  • Air, hydraulic and fuel systems for leaks
  • Loose or missing parts
  • Cables, wiring harnesses and G.F.I. outlets
  • Placards, warnings, control markings and operating manuals
  • Guardrail system including locking pins
  • Engine oil level (if applicable)
  • Battery fluid level
  • Hydraulic reservoir level
  • Coolant level (if applicable)
