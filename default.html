WELCOME TO THORVALDSON APPRAISAL LTD.
Thorvaldson Appraisal Ltd. is an independent real estate consulting and valuation company specializing in commercial and agricultural property. Darrell Thorvaldson is an accredited member of the Appraisal Institute of Canada. Thorvaldson Appraisal Ltd. offers services to assist successful business decisions including point in time appraisals and market analysis reports. Litigation support is offered with advisory roles in expropriation, foreclosure, estate settlement and expert witness.
