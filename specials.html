September Specials!

10% off CAO brand cigars and more

During the month of September we are happy to offer our entire selection of CAO brand cigars at 10% off. These cigars, with their variety of strengths and flavours, have been steadily gaining in popularity over the last couple of years. If you haven’t had a chance to try them yet now is the time!
