About Us


Clearview Rentals is a local scissor lift rental agency serving the Manitoba region. We rent our lifts to commercial and industrial contractors on a daily, weekly and monthly basis. Our fleet is made up of a range of high quality Skyjack machines, each suitable for various customer needs. We provide electric, gas and propane models to accommodate requirements for specific jobs. We offer the option of 2W and 4W drive and have machines with non-marking tires suitable for indoor use. Our goal is to provide excellent service by doing whatever it takes to help you get what you need and when you need it.

Safety Training
Programs

Clearview Rentals offers a lift operator training certification for aerial lifts.


Safety is extremely important to us and we would like to ensure that all users feel safe and comfortable on our lifts. We provide training for both electric and rough terrain lifts. Each 3-hour course consists of both lift theory and operation. Trainees will be expected to watch a video, followed by hands-on training with our certified lift instructor.

Contact us to inquire about training dates.
