Welcome to Thomas Hinds

For 20 years Thomas Hinds has been Winnipeg's premier Tobacconist. Through the quality of our products and the excellence of our customer service we have built a shopping experience that is second to none.

Visit our walk in cigar humidor for the best Cuban and non-Cuban cigars including the world renowned Thomas Hinds Selection cigars. We also offer a diverse amount of tobacco accessories from quality pipes to cigar humidors to meet the needs of even the most particular tobacco afficianado.
