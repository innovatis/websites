Welcome to Thomas Hinds

For 20 years Thomas Hinds Tobacconist has been Winnipeg's premier source for quality cigars, tobacco, and related accessories. Through the excellence of our customer service, and our knowledgeable staff, we have built a shopping experience that is second to none for both our local customers, and those who may be visiting our city. Should you be passing through on Sunday, when we are closed, please feel free to contact us to make arrangements for delivery to your hotel, or destination.

Visit our walk in cigar humidor for the best Cuban and non-Cuban cigars, including the world-renowned Thomas Hinds Selection of Honduran and Nicaraguan cigars. The pipe smoker is presented with a variety of tobaccos from around the world, in both tinned and bulk blends. We also offer a broad selection of related accessories. Quality pipes, cigar humidors, hand made pens, and shaving supplies are all available to meet the needs of even the most particular aficionado.
