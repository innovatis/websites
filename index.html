About us

Marc and Deborah are detail oriented, work extremely hard for our clients, and try to make the experience as enjoyable and stress free as possible. The Meyers Team provides honest and ethical advice, timely feedback, plus exceptional customer service. (As evidenced by client referral business, continued relationships, and recent testimonials.)

Marc and Deborah are very patient and never pressure clients into making a decision until they're ready. Of course, once the right property is found, they will negotiate hard to get the best price and dates possible. Lastly, they'll stay on top of the transaction to ensure a smooth close, and follow up after the completion date.

"Marc & Deborah take pride in their level of professionalism and service, and understand that an increasingly educated and informed public deserves and expects only the highest degree of performance."
