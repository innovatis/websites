Safety Training
Programs

Clearview Rentals offers a lift operator training certification for aerial lifts.


Safety is extremely important to us and we would like to ensure that all users feel safe and comfortable on our lifts. We provide training for both electric and rough terrain lifts. Each 3-hour course consists of both lift theory and operation. Trainees will be expected to watch a video, followed by hands-on training with our certified lift instructor.

Contact us to inquire about training dates.
