We supply new, recycled, and rebuilt car and truck parts

We specialize in new, recycled and rebuilt parts. Our original location opened in 1962. It specializes in supplying quality recycled car and truck parts.

Our Winnipeg location opened on Wall Street in 1991, supplying new parts and a complete automotive service facility. Our service facility specializes in engine and transmission work.

Direct Auto Parts employs over 25 full-time employees, and is a member of the following organizations:

  • Automotive Industries Association (AIA) of Canada
  • Automotive Recyclers of Manitoba (ARM)
  • Automotive Traders Association (ATA)
  • Automotive Recyclers Association (ARA) of Canada
  • Automotive Parts Rebuilders Association (APRA)
  • Canadian Federation of Independent Business (CFIB)
