About Us

Since 1991, Thomas Hinds Tobacconist in Winnipeg has been committed to achieving excellence in customer service and quality of product. We strive to provide our clients with that personal touch that helps to make shopping for our products a pleasurable experience.

Our walk in cigar humidor offers the best Cuban and non-Cuban cigars including the world renowned Thomas Hinds Selection cigars. We also provide a diverse selection of premium pipe tobaccos.

Our store also provides a diverse amount of tobacco accessories from quality pipes to cigar humidors to meet any customer requirement.

We also feature non-tobacco items including writing instruments, grooming accessories and Lampe Berger products.
