PERSONAL COACHING

There are times when individual or family circumstances may have an impact on business productivity and ownership responsibilities. Val works with individuals to provide personal support. Her process succeeds in minimizing the negative impact on business, while individual health and wellness is strengthened. Some scenarios that clients have accessed Val's private and confidential support include:

  • divorce
  • death
  • addictions
  • illness
  • disenchantment
  • depression
  • stress and burn-out
  • Asperger's Syndrome
  • gender identity
  • change & transitions
